Welcome to Woxsen Campus Mart, powered by the AI Research Centre! Our website page is designed to facilitate the buying and selling of pre-owned products exclusively within the vibrant Woxsen campus community.

At Woxsen Campus Mart, we understand the value of sustainability and the importance of giving pre-owned items a second life. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or staff member at Woxsen, this platform provides a convenient and efficient way to connect with others on campus who are interested in buying or selling their gently used items.

Our main objective is to foster a culture of resourcefulness, affordability, and community engagement within the Woxsen campus. By encouraging the exchange of second-hand products, we not only contribute to a greener environment by reducing waste but also promote a sense of camaraderie among the Woxsen community members.

How does it work? As a registered user of Woxsen Campus Mart, you can create listings to sell your pre-owned items. Simply upload clear images, provide detailed descriptions, set a reasonable price, and categorize your item appropriately. Potential buyers within the Woxsen campus can browse through the listings and directly connect with you to negotiate the purchase.

On the other hand, if you're looking to buy pre-owned products, you can explore the wide variety of listings available on Woxsen Campus Mart. From textbooks and electronics to furniture and clothing, you'll find a diverse range of items that cater to your needs and budget. Connect with sellers, inquire about the condition of the items, and negotiate prices to strike a deal.
